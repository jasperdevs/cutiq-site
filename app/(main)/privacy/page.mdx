## Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

CutIQ ("CutIQ," "we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered hair analysis, hairstyle previews, routines, and chat-based hair guidance on iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## Overview

CutIQ is designed to help you analyze your hair, preview hairstyles, and track progress over time. To do that, the app uses a combination of on-device data, Apple's services, and our backend infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use third-party advertising trackers inside the app.

## Data We Collect

CutIQ may process the following categories of information:

- **Photos and image content.** If you take a photo in the app or choose one from your photo library, CutIQ uses that image to generate hairstyle previews or hair analysis results.
- **Hair analysis and routine data.** The app stores your analysis results, routine preferences, streaks, settings, and related progress information.
- **Identifiers.** CutIQ uses an iCloud / CloudKit-based user identity and app-specific identifiers needed for syncing data and verifying secure app requests.
- **Purchase and subscription status.** If you subscribe, the app verifies whether you have an active entitlement through Apple in-app purchases.
- **Usage-limiting and security metadata.** Our backend may process limited request metadata needed for App Attest verification, subscription validation, abuse prevention, and rate limiting.
- **Support communications.** If you email us, we receive the information you include in that message.

## How We Use Data

We use data to:

- provide hairstyle generation, hair analysis, and chat features;
- save your app state and progress across sessions;
- sync your information through your personal iCloud / CloudKit storage where applicable;
- verify subscriptions and secure app requests;
- enforce usage limits and help prevent abuse;
- improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and respond to support requests.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as needed to operate the app, including with the service providers listed below, or when required by law.

## Third-Party Services

CutIQ uses the following third-party and platform services:

1. **Apple CloudKit / iCloud** by Apple - used for syncing certain app data and account state.
   - Data involved: profile and routine settings, analysis-related app state, progress data, and other app records you create.
   - Apple privacy policy: [Apple privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

2. **Apple In-App Purchase / App Store** by Apple - used to process subscriptions and provide purchase status.
   - Data involved: transaction and subscription status metadata.
   - We do not receive your full payment card information.
   - Apple privacy policy: [Apple privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

3. **Supabase** by Supabase, Inc. - used for backend edge functions that power secure app verification, hair analysis requests, hairstyle generation requests, and chat requests.
   - Data involved: request payloads needed to fulfill those features, including image data you submit for analysis or generation, secure request verification metadata, and subscription verification metadata.
   - Supabase privacy policy: [Supabase privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

4. **Superwall** by Superwall - used to manage paywall presentation and entitlement handling.
   - Data involved: app-specific user identifiers and subscription / entitlement state needed to determine access.
   - Superwall privacy policy: [Superwall privacy policy](https://superwall.com/privacy)

5. **ModelRouter / AI model providers accessed through our backend** - used to produce hair analysis, hairstyle generation, and chat responses.
   - Data involved: the text and image content you submit for those features.
   - These requests are sent through our backend rather than directly from your device.

## Data Retention

- Data stored in your personal Apple iCloud / CloudKit account may remain there until you delete it, delete app data, or remove it through your Apple account and device settings.
- Backend request data is retained only as long as reasonably needed to operate the service, secure the app, troubleshoot issues, and comply with legal obligations.
- Support emails are retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain support history.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information handled by CutIQ. These measures include encrypted network transport, Apple App Attest verification for supported devices, subscription verification, and backend controls intended to reduce abuse and unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- stop granting camera or photo-library access through your iPhone settings;
- disable notifications through iPhone settings or within the app;
- manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple;
- contact us to request help related to your data or privacy.

If you want certain synced app data removed, contact us at support@cutiq.app and include enough information for us to understand your request.

## Children's Privacy

CutIQ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Transfers

Depending on where you use the app and where our service providers operate, your information may be processed in countries other than your own.

## App Tracking Transparency

CutIQ does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, CutIQ may schedule local notifications on your device for hair tips, routine reminders, and weekly check-ins. These notifications are managed through Apple notification services and your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

## Contact Us

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact us at [support@cutiq.app](mailto:support@cutiq.app).
